Jee is an experience designer and developer, who was raised in Seoul and Chicago. She started off hoping to become a museum curator, but later studied how the intersections in art and technology influenced design thinking, and vice versa, at Wellesley College and MIT.

She hopes to use her creativity and lateral thinking to find solutions that deliver more emotional context and contemplate on how design is implemented across various cultures and platforms. Good design shouldn’t exclude but should celebrate diversity.

In her free time, other than designing for freelance, she loves to code for art, screen print, binge watch Adult Swim, and waits till the day she meets Aphex Twin.
